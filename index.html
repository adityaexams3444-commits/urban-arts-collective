About the Society

The Corporate & Commercial Law Society (CCLS) at Campus Law Centre, Faculty of Law, University of Delhi is a student-driven academic initiative dedicated to fostering awareness, research, and professional engagement in the field of corporate and commercial law. The Society was established with the vision of bridging the gap between theoretical legal education and the practical realities of the corporate legal profession, enabling students to develop both strong legal foundations and industry-relevant skills.

CCLS provides a dynamic platform for students to explore diverse areas of corporate law through lectures, seminars, workshops, training sessions, and conferences conducted by industry experts, alumni, and legal practitioners. These initiatives aim to equip students with practical insights into the functioning of corporate legal practice while encouraging analytical thinking and professional development.

The Society also promotes academic discourse and research through its online blog and journal, which serve as platforms for students to publish and exchange ideas on contemporary developments in corporate and commercial law. By fostering collaboration between students, faculty, alumni, and legal professionals, CCLS seeks to create a vibrant community committed to learning, innovation, and excellence in corporate legal practice.

Through its initiatives, the Society strives to nurture the next generation of corporate lawyers by providing meaningful opportunities for learning, networking, and professional growth.
